Hunza Valley

Hunza Valley is one of Pakistan's most beautiful tourist destinations. Located in Gilgit-Baltistan, it is famous for its majestic mountains, breathtaking landscapes, and rich culture.

Image of Hunza Valley

About Hunza

Hunza Valley is surrounded by some of the world's highest peaks, including Rakaposhi, Ultar Sar, and Ladyfinger Peak. The valley is known for its peaceful environment and welcoming people.

Top Attractions

  • Baltit Fort
  • Altit Fort
  • Attabad Lake
  • Passu Cones
  • Khunjerab Pass

Popular Activities

  • Mountain Hiking
  • Photography
  • Camping
  • Boating at Attabad Lake
  • Sightseeing

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Hunza is from April to October. Spring offers beautiful cherry blossoms, while autumn provides stunning golden landscapes.
