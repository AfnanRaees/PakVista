Explore Hunza Valley

Hunza Valley is one of Pakistan's most beautiful tourist destinations, located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region. It is famous for its breathtaking mountains, lakes, and cultural heritage.

Image of Hunza Valley

About Hunza

Hunza Valley is surrounded by majestic peaks such as Rakaposhi, Ultar Sar, and Ladyfinger Peak. The valley attracts thousands of tourists every year due to its natural beauty and peaceful environment.

Top Tourist Attractions

  • Baltit Fort
  • Altit Fort
  • Attabad Lake
  • Passu Cones
  • Khunjerab Pass

Things to Do

  • Mountain Hiking
  • Camping
  • Photography
  • Boating at Attabad Lake
  • Sightseeing

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Hunza Valley is from April to October. Spring offers beautiful cherry blossoms, while autumn provides colorful golden landscapes.
